About Us

Learn about our mission, history, and role in the school

Our Vision

To cultivate a generation of creative and humanitarian media personalities equipped with exceptional communication skills.

Our Mission

To empower an exemplary student body that leads with teamwork and responsibility, remains fortified with media literacy and critical thinking, and serves the nation with ethical accountability.

Our Role in the School

As the official media organization of DS Senanayake Central College Mirigama, we are responsible for:

  • Carrying out announcement duties during school events and ceremonies
  • Hosting and conducting the morning assembly
  • Managing and hosting other school meetings and official gatherings
  • Handling sound system and audio management for school events on many occasions
  • Handling sound system and audio management for school events on many occasions
  • Organizing and coordinating different school events and ceremonies

Club History

Our Media Club was officially established on 23rd February 2021 and has been registered under the Government Information Department with the registration number DGI/SMC/WP/MIN/21/009

At the time of its establishment, the Media Club consisted of a leadership board of fifteen members. From its inception to the present day, the club has carried out numerous activities both within the school and beyond, contributing actively to media-related initiatives and events.

Activities Conducted Within the School

Examples of activities carried out within the school include:

  • Conducting morning broadcasting sessions
  • Providing announcements for school events and ceremonies
  • Organizing various school functions
  • Conducting an internal school announcing competition
  • Organizing a Vesak card design competition
  • Conducting a Derana media workshop
  • Organizing the “Sathimath Siphala” educational program

Activities Conducted Outside the School

Examples of activities carried out outside the school include:

  • Participation of Media Club students in various programs conducted by media institutions such as Swarnavahini, Derana, Shraddha TV, and KIDS TV
  • Supporting and managing events and ceremonies in collaboration with various schools
  • Handling announcement duties at the prize-giving ceremony held in conjunction with World Children’s Day at the Meerigama Police Station
  • Participating in workshops organized by various institutions and universities
