Annual Media Workshop 2024

April 15, 2024
Workshop

A full-day immersive workshop covering photography, videography, and digital journalism techniques.

Workshop Overview

Our Annual Media Workshop is the flagship event of the School Media Club, designed to equip students with practical skills in media production and journalism. This year's workshop featured industry professionals who shared their expertise through hands-on sessions and interactive demonstrations.

Workshop Sessions

  • Digital Photography Fundamentals - Learn camera settings, composition techniques, and lighting basics
  • Video Production & Editing - Hands-on training with video cameras and editing software
  • Journalism Ethics & Reporting - Understanding responsible journalism and interview techniques
  • Social Media Content Creation - Creating engaging content for various social platforms
  • Live Event Coverage - Practical exercise covering a mock school event

Learning Outcomes

Participants gained practical experience with professional equipment, learned how to tell compelling stories through different media formats, and developed skills that will serve them in both academic and professional settings. The workshop emphasized hands-on learning, with students producing their own content throughout the day.

Featured Guest Instructors

This year's workshop featured Sarah Miller, award-winning photojournalist from The City Times, and Michael Chen, documentary filmmaker and DS Senanayake Central College Mirigama alumnus. Their real-world experience provided invaluable insights into media careers.
