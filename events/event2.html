Spring Edition: School Newspaper Launch

March 28, 2024
Publication

Launch of "The Lincoln Chronicle" Spring 2024 edition featuring student journalism and photography.

Newspaper Launch Event

The Media Club proudly launched the Spring 2024 edition of "The Lincoln Chronicle," our quarterly school newspaper. This edition features 32 pages of student-written articles, photography, and illustrations covering school events, student achievements, and community issues.

Featured Articles

  • "The Future of School Athletics" by Sarah Johnson - An in-depth look at upcoming sports programs
  • "Climate Action in Our Community" by Daniel Park - Student-led environmental initiatives
  • "Artists Among Us" by Emily Rodriguez - Profiles of talented student artists
  • "Technology in Education" by Michael Chen - How digital tools are transforming learning
  • "Mental Health Awareness" by Sophia Williams - Resources and support for students

Launch Celebration

The launch event was attended by students, faculty, and parents. Club members distributed copies of the newspaper and discussed their articles with readers. Principal Thompson commended the Media Club for their professional work and dedication to school journalism.

Digital Edition

For the first time, this edition is also available in digital format on the school website. The digital edition includes interactive elements, video interviews, and photo galleries that complement the print articles.
