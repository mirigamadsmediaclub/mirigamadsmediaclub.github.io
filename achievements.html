Achievements

Learn about our achievements and recognition in school media activities

Our Vision

To cultivate a generation of creative and humanitarian media personalities equipped with exceptional communication skills.

Our Mission

To empower an exemplary student body that leads with teamwork and responsibility, remains fortified with media literacy and critical thinking, and serves the nation with ethical accountability.

Our Role in the School

As the official media organization of DS Senanayake Central College Mirigama, we are responsible for:

  • Documenting school events, assemblies, and competitions
  • Producing the quarterly school newspaper
  • Creating multimedia content for school publications
  • Managing the school's official social media accounts
  • Providing media coverage for sports and cultural events
  • Assisting with yearbook production

Club History

The Media Club was established in 2010 by a group of students passionate about journalism and photography. What began as a small photography club has grown into a comprehensive media organization with over 50 active members. Over the years, we have expanded our equipment, skills, and reach within the school community.

In 2018, we took over management of the school's social media presence, and in 2020, we began producing the school newspaper in digital format. Today, we continue to adapt to new technologies while maintaining our commitment to documenting DS Senanayake Central College Mirigama's story.
